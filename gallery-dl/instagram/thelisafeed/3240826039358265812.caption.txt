Wagyu IDATEN
Japan, 〒542-0076 Osaka, Chuo Ward, Nanba, 1 Chome−8−20 嘉光ビル 2階

Located right in the central Namba area before you hit dotonbori. The entrance is rather obscure so look out for the sign telling you to head up the elevator to level 2!

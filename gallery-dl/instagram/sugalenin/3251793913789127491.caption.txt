When Tokyo gets too crowded, I hop on a train or bus to Chiba Prefecture 🌊
Grabbing a drink and snack from the local convenience store, I just sit back and watch the surfers ride the waves, soaking in the sunlight. 
📍Onjuku town/御宿町is this small, charming spot – crazy busy in summer, but wonderfully empty in winter. If you’re into solitude, watching the tides, and just chilling without any plans, this place is exactly what you need. 
By the way, it’s not cold at all if it’s sunny. Just make sure to bring a hat and sunglasses to fully enjoy the winter sun!

Access from Tokyo:
🚌there is a direct bus departing from Tokyo Station Yaesu Exit station and arriving at Onjuku. Services depart three times a day, and operate every day. The journey takes approximately 2hours
🚞there is a direct train departing from Tōkyō Station station and arriving at Onjuku. Services depart every three hours, and operate every day. The journey takes approximately 1h 20m.

東京が混雑しすぎると、電車に乗って千葉県に向かいます🌊
近くのコンビニで飲み物とお菓子を手に入れ、波に乗るサーファーたちを見ながら、太陽の光を浴びてくつろぎます。
📍御宿町はこの小さくて魅力的なスポットで、夏は賑やかで、冬は静かです。もし静けさや潮の流れを眺め、何も予定を立てずにリラックスしたいなら、この場所がぴったりです。
ところで、晴れていると全然寒くないんですよ。ただし、冬の太陽を十分に楽しむためには帽子とサングラスを持参するのを忘れずに！

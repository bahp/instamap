📍Kurama, Kyoto( 鞍馬 / 京都 )

The Kurama Fire Festival is one of the famous festivals in Kyoto, which takes place annually on October 22nd.

Originating from over a thousand years ago, when villagers welcomed the local deity with numerous torches, this tradition still continues today.

The sight of the town filled with torches was like Sengoku era🔥

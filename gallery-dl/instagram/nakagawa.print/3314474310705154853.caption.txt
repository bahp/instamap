Itsukushima Shrine has a divine crest of ``three-layered double tortoiseshell with a sword and flower bishi'', and the sword and flowerbishi are used in the turtleshell crest, which means Genbu (turtle and snake), the guardians of the north. Itsukushima Shrine is said to be dedicated to the ``Three Goddesses of Munakata'' or ``Three Goddess.''

If you’re in Osaka and want to see more of Japan’s countryside, riding the Tamaden (cat-themed train) on the Wakayama Electric Railway Kishigawa Line is a great option for you to do so. 🐈 🚃 🇯🇵

The scenic train line is located about 1-1.5 hours from Osaka Station.

LOCATIONS: 

📍Wakayama Electric Railway - Kishigawa Line
📍Kishi Station // Tama Station

Do you know there is another Mt.Fuji in Japan😍

This is Mt.Yotei in Niseko, Hokkaido, Japan! It looks super similar to Mt.Fuji! 

It is really easy to take pictures here since there are not many people and Hokkaido has beautiful wide landscape makes it less crowded🤍

You do not need any camera with telephoto lens to take pictures or videos here! I took this with my iPhone 12 Pro with 2x zoom! And the mountain it self looks huge in real life so don’t worry, anyone can take beautiful shots here💕

The exact shooting location in this reel: 52 Makkari, Abuta District, Hokkaido 048-1631

這裡是位在北海道二世谷的羊蹄山！是不是跟富士山長得很像😍這裡人很少超級好拍！來北海道的時候一定要來這裡走走！

Do you know there is another Mt.Fuji in Japan😍

This is not Mt.Fuji! This is Mt.Yotei in Niseko, Hokkaido, Japan! It looks super similar to Mt.Fuji and is known by name: Ezo Fuji which means Hokkaido’s Fuji. 

It is really easy to take pictures here since there are not many people and Hokkaido has beautiful wide landscape makes it less crowded🤍

You do not need any camera with telephoto lens to take pictures or videos here! I took this with my iPhone 12 Pro with 2x zoom!

The exact shooting location in this reel: 52 Makkari, Abuta District, Hokkaido 048-1631

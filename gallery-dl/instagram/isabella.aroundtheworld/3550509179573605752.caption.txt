The Dolomites are a MUST for all the mountain lovers out there and exploring region on a roadtrip is definitely the best way! 😍

Here’s a first timer’s Dolomites itinerary with some of the most beautiful spots and some optional, lesser known places to check out as well! 

Day 1️⃣

- Cadinini di Misurina viewpoint
- Tre Cime di Lavaredo
- Lago di Misurina

other optional stops:
- Lago de Landro
- Lago Antorno

Day 2️⃣:

- Cortina d’Ampezzo
- Lago di Sorapis
- Lago di Carezza

other optional stops:
- Lago Federa
- Lago Limides
- Passo Giau

Day 3️⃣:

- St. Valentin’s church
- Castelrotto
- Alpe di Siusi

other optional stops:
- St. Konstantin’s church

Day 4️⃣

- Seceda
- Cascate de Pisciadu
- Gardena Pass

other optional stops not in the video:

- St. Jacob’s church

Day 5️⃣:

- Val di Funes
- Earth pyramids of Platten
- Lago di Braies

other optional stops:
- Würzjoch
- Lago di Dobbiaco

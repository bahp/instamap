Street Ramen Stall in Tokyo

The experience of enjoying a steaming bowl of ramen while watching trains pass by adds a unique touch to the meal, making it even more memorable.

The ramen not only looks visually appealing but also boasts a delightful flavor, with thin noodles perfectly soaking up the savory soy sauce broth. Everything here is made from scratch, and the broth is crafted with dashi and chicken stock without any additives, ensuring a natural and wholesome taste.

- Menu: Chashu Ramen (950 yen), Ajitama Egg (100 yen extra)

As a mountainous country, Japan has a milieu of lush natural offerings, including the mineral-rich hot springs that swell from Earth’s crust. With views that change with the seasons and waters of varying characteristics, Japan’s onsen can be enjoyed year-round and cross-country.⁠
⁠
The charm of an onsen doesn’t end with the waters either as most popular hot spring destinations have plenty of exploring and touristic opportunities available.⁠

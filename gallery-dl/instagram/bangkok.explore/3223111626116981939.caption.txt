Wat Arun’s architectural artistry reflects the essence of Thai culture. 🎨🏯

💡: Wat Arun is often referred to as the “Temple of Dawn.” The name is derived from Aruna, the Hindu god of dawn, and it symbolizes the first light of day that reflects off the temple’s surface.✨

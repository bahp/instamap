COFTOALCO 
⁡
Curry shop in Nagoya where you can order cute tiramisu balls🍰
⁡
They have 2 flavors espresso and matcha, both served alongside extra sauce.
⁡
If you pour the sauce before eating, it’ll slide right off just like the video, so I would recommend using it after eating a few bites.
⁡
The tiramisu is delicious on its own and I especially liked the matcha flavor🍵
Both flavors are 600yen.
⁡
Their curry looked good too, so next time I’ll have to try them🍛
⁡
Recommended!

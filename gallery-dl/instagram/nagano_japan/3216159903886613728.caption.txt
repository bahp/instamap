Narai-juku
(Shiojiri City)

The atmosphere of the Edo Period wafts through the street of Narai-juku lined with traditional wooden inns.

From mid-October to mid-November, autumn leaves take hold in the valley, further bringing out the town’s character.

Wouldn’t you like to walk along this ancient townscape and slip back in time to the Edo Period?

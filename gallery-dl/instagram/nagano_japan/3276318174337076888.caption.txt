Kurumayama Shrine
(Chino City)

Kuruyama Shrine sits atop Mt. Kurumayama, the tallest peak of Kirigamine Kogen and one of Japan’s top 100.

The shrine seems to float in the sky thanks to the 360-degree panoramic views here.

The shrine and its four wooden pillars, thrust into the sky, are a symbol of Kurumayama.

*The road to Kirigamine may be covered in snow or ice. When visiting Kirigamine, be sure to use winter tires, bring tire chains and drive carefully. Also, please only park in designated parking spaces and refrain from entering prohibited areas.

🔘The Hanging Temple🔘
⚫Located in Datong City, Shanxi Province, China.
⚫The Hanging Temple is one of the main tourist attractions and historical sites in the Datong area.
⚫According to legend, construction of the temple started at the end of the Northern Wei dynasty (386-534) AD by only one man, a monk named Liaoran and was restored in the 1900th.
⚫The entire 40 halls and pavilions are all built on cliffs which are over 30 metres (98 ft) from the ground.
⚫Interestingly, the temple is dedicated to not just one religion, but three, with Confucianism, Taoism, and Buddhism all practised within the temple and represented in 78 statues and carvings throughout the temple.

💡 The Northern Lights, or Aurora Borealis, are vivid natural light displays that occur in the polar regions, including Iceland and Finland. Caused by solar wind interacting with Earth's atmosphere, they create stunning green, pink, and purple hues dancing across the night sky, attracting tourists and photographers alike to witness this mesmerizing phenomenon.

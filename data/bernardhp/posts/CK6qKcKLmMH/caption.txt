🔵Whitby Abbey🔵
⚫The 7th-century Christian monastery was situated on the East Cliff above Whitby in North Yorkshire, England.
⚫The ruins of the abbey have been used by sailors as a landmark at the headland.
🧛‍♂️The famous author" Bram Stoker "got the inspiration for his epic novel "Dracula" from the colossal remains of Whitby Abbey.
👻There is also a legend about a pale ghost of a lady who wanders in the shadowy ruins of the monastery.
😨The legend says that the lady was bricked up alive in one of the walls and that she was frequently seen in one of the crumbled windows.
⚫Since the 20th century, the substantial ruins of the church have been declared a Grade I Listed building and are in the care of English Heritage;the site museum is housed in Cholmley House.
